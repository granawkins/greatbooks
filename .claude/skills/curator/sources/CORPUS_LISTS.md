## Source Notes

- **Modern Library**: 1998 board vote, novels only, predominantly 20th century → most NOT PD
- **Gutenberg Top 100**: reflects current download demand, strong proxy for what readers actually want
- **thegreatestbooks.org**: aggregates ~30 lists (Modern Library, Time, Guardian, Le Monde, etc.); top titles cluster around same works — Tolstoy, Joyce, Austen, Dickens, Dostoevsky
- **Penguin Classics** as brand signal: if it's a Penguin Classic, it's canonical and has a market
